WHAT IS CONTAINER

24/11/2021, created by rahmad

container refeer

A refrigerated container or reefer is an intermodal container (shipping container) used in intermodal freight transport that is capable of refrigeration for the transportation of temperature-sensitive, perishable cargo such as fruits, vegetables, meat, and other similar items. did u know that the price of container isn't determined by the size but the function of the customer itself. example 2010 until now the highest rate to bought countainer is 20 size not 40, why? because the 20 RF (refeer) is easy to place it and doesn't take too much space . this is why people buy 20 size

container standard(shipping container)

A shipping container or often called standard container is an intermodal container that designed and built for intermodal freight transport, meaning these containers can be used across different modes of transport – from ship to rail to truck – without unloading and reloading their cargo. shipping containers are primarily used to store and transport materials and products efficiently and securely in the global containerized intermodal freight transport system. intermodal transport is a system of transporting freight in one unit of transport vehicles can be in form container truck or cargo.(translate) shipping container atau di sebut container standard adalah sebuat intermoda container yang didesign dan diibentuk untuk kendaran pengangkutan barang, yang maksudnya bisa di gunakan dalam berbeda kendaraan dari kapal ke kereta ke truk tanpa bongkar dan muat ulang cargo. shipping container biasanya digunakan untuk menyimpan dan mengirim material dan products secara efesient dan terjaga dalam sistem angkutan barang antar moda peti kemas global.
